### 🧠 Concept & Methodology
Developed by **Oleg Dolgoarshinnykh**, this framework is the technical implementation of the **AI Brand Binding** methodology. In highly regulated YMYL (Your Money, Your Life) industries like Healthcare and Addiction Rehab, traditional advertising is often restricted. 

This engine solves the "visibility gap" by building a dense semantic architecture that makes a brand a **Benchmark Entity** within global Knowledge Graphs, ensuring dominance in generative search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini).

---

### 🛠 Technical Specifications & Functional Modules (20+ Logic Blocks)

The `semantic_processor.php` acts as a high-level orchestrator for enterprise-scale structured data.

#### 1. Clinical Authority & E-E-A-T Core
*   **MedicalCondition Orchestrator:** Deep mapping of clinical conditions to specific evidence-based treatment modalities.
*   **MedicalWebPage Specialist:** Automated transformation of standard HTML pages into high-trust clinical entities for search crawlers.
*   **Expert Reviewer Binding:** Programmatic linking of medical professionals (doctors/clinicians) to content, including license verification and NPI signaling.
*   **MedicalGuideline Integration:** Semantic signaling of adherence to international clinical protocols and treatment standards.
*   **Evidence-Based Content Mapping:** Logical bridging of clinical studies and peer-reviewed citations to service claims.

#### 2. Entity Relationship Architecture (Knowledge Graph)
*   **Recursive Breadcrumb Mapping:** Generates semantic hierarchy paths that define site taxonomy for LLM/AI crawlers.
*   **Organization-to-Facility Linking:** Manages complex relationships between corporate brand entities and physical medical locations.
*   **Service-to-Program Alignment:** Detailed mapping of specific "Treatment Programs" as standalone professional service entities.
*   **FAQ Entity Binding:** Transforms unstructured data into Knowledge Graph nodes for AI snippet generation.
*   **SameAs Authority Linking:** Hard-coding connections to global identifiers (Wikidata, ORCID, Official Medical Registers).

#### 3. Growth & Conversion Logic (GEO)
*   **Review/Rating Aggregation Engine:** Structured orchestration of patient outcomes and success metrics to build trust signals.
*   **Insurance & Offer Logic:** Managed visibility for complex financial data, insurance coverage, and private-pay entities.
*   **Emergency ContactPoint Logic:** Prioritizes emergency signals for AI-agent "Near Me" and urgent care queries.
*   **Video/Media Semantic Wrapping:** Contextualizing facility tours and clinical testimonials for visual and multimodal AI search.
*   **Dynamic Intake Orchestration:** Logical handling of cohort dates and program availability signals.

#### 4. Architecture & Security (System Level)
*   **Enterprise Data Sanitization:** Advanced filtering for massive semantic payloads (26k+ character processing).
*   **JSON-LD Minification & Injection:** High-speed delivery system designed to maintain 100/100 Core Web Vitals.
*   **Context-Aware Metadata Generation:** Dynamic generation of meta-signals based on the semantic depth of the entity.
*   **Schema.org Version Alignment:** Ensuring 100% compatibility with the latest Schema.org v23.0+ vocabularies.
*   **Intelligent Fallback Logic:** Maintaining graph integrity even when primary clinical data points are missing.
*   **Extensible Hook System:** Allows for custom entity injection without modifying the core semantic engine.
